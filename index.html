About me


Vincent Yang (or Vince) loves technology. At only the age of 10, ever since "helping" his cousin build a computer for a family member, Vince knew that his future was in tech. From designing fiber optic cable infrastructure, engineering 5G cell towers, building computers, repairing and restoring cell-phones, to cable management: he's had a hand in it. Vince is currently a student in Georgia Tech's coding Boot Camp program.


Work History

Vince has experience in many fields within the tech workforce:

  • Outside plant fiber infrastructure design - AutoCAD and BSTCAD - 2017-2022
  • Engineering Project Management deploying 5G small cell nodes in GA - 2019-2020
  • Program director of the Uber Xchange Leasing Program for Honda - 2015-2016
  • Cell phone image flashing/burning and complete repair for MetroPCS- 2011-2014
